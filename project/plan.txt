## Coordinate System
- The origin (0, 0, 0) is placed at the **bottom-left corner of the Garage**.
- The X-axis represents horizontal movement (right is positive).
- The Y-axis represents vertical movement (up is positive).
- All dimensions are in meters.
- Wall thickness is assumed to be **0.50m** for external walls and **0.10m** for internal walls.
